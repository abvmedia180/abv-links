The Precedent

This isn't a new idea.

charity: water · est. 2006

Two separate accounts. One rule. Every public dollar goes to the water project.

Scott Harrison's 100% model raised hundreds of millions of dollars on exactly this structure. The architecture is now taught as a case study at Harvard Business School and the Stanford Graduate School of Business. Kind Steward applies the same proven model — tuned for a two-entity legal structure.
