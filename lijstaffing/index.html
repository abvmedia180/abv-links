    Staffing Optimizer - Quick Guide

    Everything here is based on real visitor volume data from security logs.

    What Does "Good Staffing" Look Like?

    Health Score 80+ = You're covering demand well. Under 60 means significant gaps.
    Peak Coverage 90%+ = Busy hours are staffed. This is the most important number.
    Utilization 75-95% = Staff are being used efficiently without burning out.
    Zero gap hours = Every hour that needs coverage has someone assigned.

    Quick Start: What Should I Do First?

    1. Pick your entry point from the dropdown (top right) -- Katz Lobby, Emergency Dept, etc.
    2. Look at the Optimized Schedule tab -- this is the AI's recommendation for the best schedule.
    3. Want to customize? Go to Schedule Builder and drag people around, or click Auto-Fill to start from the recommendation.
    4. Use Insights to test "What if?" scenarios -- add more staff, change volume, extend hours.

    How to Edit and Customize

    Rename employees: Click any employee name on the Schedule tab to type a real name.
    Add staff: Click + FT (full-time) or + PT (part-time) above the Team panel.
    Remove staff: Click the x button on any employee card.
    Change hourly rate: Edit the $/hr field on the cost card to update all cost calculations.
    Build your own schedule: On the Builder tab, drag employees onto time slots, or use the Morning/Afternoon/Evening shift buttons.

    Color Coding

    Green cells = Low volume. 1 staff member is enough.
    Yellow cells = Moderate volume. 1-2 staff recommended.
    Red cells = High volume. 2-3 staff needed to keep up.

    The 4 Tabs

    Optimized Schedule -- The recommended schedule based on visitor patterns. Shows who works when, coverage gaps, and cost. Toggle Week A/B for weekend rotation.
    Volume Analysis -- When do visitors actually come? Heatmaps and charts showing traffic patterns by hour and day of week.
    Schedule Builder -- Your sandbox. Drag and drop employees into shifts. Red borders show where you're short-staffed. Save your schedule or export to CSV.
    Insights & What-If -- Performance metrics and a simulator. Slide the controls to answer questions like "What if we add 1 more person?" or "What if volume goes up 20%?"
    Note: All data is based on actual security desk visitor logs from August 2025 onward. Employee names and custom schedules are saved in your browser only. Printing creates a clean report. Data refreshes when new visitor logs are processed.
