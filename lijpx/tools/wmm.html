What Matters
Most to You?

Your voice matters — because your care should reflect your values.

At Northwell Health, we understand that the little things often make the biggest difference. That’s why, throughout your stay, a member of your care team will regularly ask:

“What matters most to you?”

Whether it’s about your medical treatment, personal comfort, family concerns, or emotional well-being — your response helps us shape your experience around what truly matters to you.

Personalized Care, Every Step of the Way

By understanding your priorities, we deliver care that’s not only clinically excellent — but deeply personal and meaningful.

Thank you for choosing Northwell Health.

We’re honored to care for you — and committed to supporting you every step of the way.

Staff Reminder

Remember to Ask

“What matters most to you?”

Ask at every interaction
During rounding, shift changes, and care transitions.
Listen without rushing
Give the patient space to share what’s on their mind.
Document their response
Note it in the care plan so the whole team knows.
Follow through
Act on what the patient shares. That’s what makes it real.
